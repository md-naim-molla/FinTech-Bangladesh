Policy Recommendations
Policy 01 · App Stability
Eliminate Timeout & Server Connectivity Errors
The 28,499-thumbs review and the majority of 1-star reviews describe the same failure: the app times out during login, bill payment, or money transfer — repeatedly and predictably. This is a systemic backend issue. Invest in server capacity scaling, CDN-based request routing, and a real-time server status page visible within the app.
🔴 Critical
Policy 02 · Authentication
Fix OTP Keyboard Bug & Add Biometric Login
87% of login/auth reviews are negative — the worst ratio of all four apps. The most common complaint is the keyboard not appearing in the OTP input field, making login literally impossible. Fix the input compatibility issue across Android versions, and introduce fingerprint/face ID as the default login to eliminate PIN dependency entirely.
🔴 Critical
Policy 03 · Registration
Rebuild the Account Opening Flow
Registration failures are cited in hundreds of reviews spanning 2021–2025 — the same bugs persisting for 4+ years. The NID photo capture flow crashes, sessions expire mid-process, and users cannot reopen where they left off. Introduce a resumable registration pipeline with local progress saving, lighter image processing, and a manual NID entry fallback.
🔴 Critical
Policy 04 · Transaction Reliability
Transaction Status Tracking & Auto-Refund SLA
512 of 943 transaction reviews are negative. Users report money deducted mid-timeout with no receipt and no recourse. Introduce a transaction ledger viewable even offline, push notifications for every state change, and a mandatory 24-hour auto-reconciliation process for failed transactions with automatic refund if unresolved.
🔴 Critical
Policy 05 · Customer Support
Build an Accessible & Responsive Support System
175 of 199 customer support reviews are negative (88%) — users report no online support channel, no response to complaints, and helpline calls that go unanswered. Launch a functional in-app chat support with a tracked ticket system, enforce a 24-hour first-response SLA, and add a Facebook Messenger bot with escalation paths.
🔴 Critical
Policy 06 · Fee Structure
Revisit Send Money Fee Tiers for Small Transactions
A highly-upvoted review (2,804 thumbs) highlights that sending 10 BDT costs 5 BDT in fees — a 50% charge. This disproportionately affects low-income and student users. Introduce a tiered micro-transaction fee structure with waived or capped charges for transactions under 50 BDT, and a monthly free-transfer quota for registered users.
🟡 Medium
Policy 07 · Device Compatibility
Expand Android Version & OEM Test Coverage
Keyboard failure, screen freezes, and crash-on-open are tied to specific Android builds and OEM manufacturers. The app must be tested against Android 8–14 across Walton, Symphony, Tecno, and Infinix — the dominant budget devices in Bangladesh. Mandate a hardware-lab regression suite before every release, with a rollback policy for critical failures.
🟡 Medium
Policy 08 · Monitoring
Real-Time Sentiment Alerting Pipeline
Monthly averages below 2.0 persisted for over a year (2023–2025) with no visible product response. Deploy an automated Play Store review monitor that triggers P0 alerts when the 7-day average drops below 2.5, auto-tags reviews by topic, and routes to the product squad within 30 minutes. Monthly sentiment reports should be mandatory board-level reading.
