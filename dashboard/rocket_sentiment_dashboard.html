Policy Recommendations
POLICY 01 · Registration & Onboarding
Overhaul the KYC & Account Registration Flow
Registration failures are the most-upvoted complaint (2,373 thumbs-up). Photo capture for NID repeatedly errors out and sessions expire mid-process. Introduce a resumable registration session, improve camera SDK for low-end devices, and allow manual NID data entry as fallback.
■ Critical Priority
POLICY 02 · Authentication
Add Biometric Login (Fingerprint / Face ID)
71% of login-related reviews are negative. PIN entry on public transit or crowded places is a security and UX concern raised repeatedly. Biometric login should be made available as the default for supported devices, with PIN as a fallback. Aligns with user requests spanning 2020–2025.
■ Critical Priority
POLICY 03 · App Stability
Structured Testing on Budget Android Devices
App Performance is the single largest topic (7,176 reviews) with a 34% negative rate. Post-update crashes on mid-to-low range Android devices (Walton, Symphony, Tecno) are recurring. Implement a mandatory device-matrix test suite covering Android 8–14 on budget OEMs before every release.
■ Critical Priority
POLICY 04 · Transaction Reliability
Transparent Transaction Status & Auto-Refund SLA
46% of Transaction/Payment reviews are negative. Users report money deducted without credit, with no clear resolution path. Introduce in-app real-time transaction status tracking, push notifications for failures, and a 48-hour automatic refund policy with visible progress updates.
■ Critical Priority
POLICY 05 · Competitive Positioning
Expand Card & Payment Partner Support
Multiple high-voted reviews highlight that Rocket lags competitors (bKash, Nagad) in accepted payment methods. Users want Dutch-Bangla Nexus Card support, Visa/Mastercard add-money, and more merchant integrations. A quarterly payment-partner expansion roadmap would directly address this gap.
◆ Medium Priority
POLICY 06 · Session & Logout Security
Implement Auto-Logout & Session Management Policy
A recurring security concern: the app stays logged in after closing, and switching accounts requires a full reinstall. Enforce auto-logout after a configurable idle period (e.g. 5 min), support multi-account switching, and add a "secure exit" option that clears session tokens locally.
◆ Medium Priority
POLICY 07 · Customer Support
Introduce In-App Issue Reporting with Ticket Tracking
Users attempting to submit feedback encounter broken feedback forms (the irony is noted in reviews). Build a functional in-app issue reporter with ticket ID generation, email confirmation, and a status tracker so users can follow up without calling the helpline.
◆ Medium Priority
POLICY 08 · Monitoring
Automated Play Store Sentiment Alerting
Rating dips below 3.5 (seen in Apr 2024) should trigger internal alerts within hours of the spike. Integrate a Play Store review monitoring pipeline that flags sudden volume increases in negative reviews, categorises by topic automatically, and routes to the responsible product squad.
● Standard Priority
