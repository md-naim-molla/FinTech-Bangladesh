Policy Recommendations
Policy 01 · Registration & KYC
Rebuild the NID Verification & Onboarding Pipeline
The most-upvoted review (3,262 thumbs-up) and multiple top-10 complaints all describe the same issue: NID photo capture fails repeatedly, sessions expire mid-process, and OTP auto-detection crashes the app. Prioritise a resumable registration flow, fallback manual NID entry, and a lighter-weight camera SDK optimised for low-end devices.
🔴 Critical Priority
Policy 02 · Authentication
Introduce Biometric Login as Default
82% of login/authentication reviews are negative — the worst ratio across all topics. Users cannot enter PINs without visibility concerns, and OTP auto-fill crashes the app on many devices. Implementing fingerprint/face ID as the primary login method with a stable PIN fallback would directly address these pain points.
🔴 Critical Priority
Policy 03 · App Stability
Structured Pre-Release Testing on Budget Devices
77% of app performance reviews are negative. Crashes occur on bill payment, recharge, and at login — often triggered by specific hardware configurations. Mandate a regression test matrix covering Android 8–14 across Walton, Symphony, Tecno, and Infinix devices before every release cycle.
🔴 Critical Priority
Policy 04 · Transaction Reliability
Real-Time Transaction Status & Auto-Refund Policy
489 of 1,693 transaction-related reviews are negative. Users report sessions expiring mid-payment, VISA add-money failures, and unexplained balance deductions with no follow-up. Introduce an in-app transaction tracker, push alerts for failures, and a 48-hour automatic refund SLA for unreconciled transactions.
🔴 Critical Priority
Policy 05 · Competitive Features
Expand Payment Methods & Partner Integrations
Users consistently compare Upay unfavourably to bKash and Nagad on feature depth — especially VISA card top-up, bank-to-bank transfer, and merchant variety. A quarterly payment-partner roadmap and API-first approach to new integrations would help close this gap and improve retention.
🟡 Medium Priority
Policy 06 · Customer Support
Build a Functional In-App Issue Reporter
The in-app feedback form was non-functional at launch — a significant trust issue. Implement a working issue reporter with auto-generated ticket IDs, email confirmation, and a status tracker. Route tickets to responsible squads by topic tag (registration, payment, login) and enforce a 72-hour first-response SLA.
🟡 Medium Priority
Policy 07 · Session Management
Fix Session Expiry & Multi-Account Handling
Session expiry mid-transaction is a recurring frustration. Users also report being unable to switch accounts without a full reinstall. Enforce session persistence for active transactions (with a 10-min inactivity auto-lock for security), and add native multi-account support in the app switcher.
🟡 Medium Priority
Policy 08 · Monitoring
Automated Sentiment Alerting & Review Dashboard
The 2021 launch crash (avg 2.61 in April) could have been caught within hours with automated alerting. Integrate a Play Store review monitoring pipeline that triggers P1 alerts when the 7-day average falls below 3.0, auto-categorises by topic, and routes to the responsible product squad — preventing repeat launch crises.
🟢 Standard Priority
